Our Story

Baked with
passion & care

Beckery began in a small Colombo kitchen with one oven, two hands, and a dream: to bring the warmth of a European artisan bakery to every doorstep. Every loaf, every bun, every crumb is made from scratch — no preservatives, no shortcuts.

We source our flour from local mills, our butter from highland dairies, and our fruits from organic farms. Because great baking starts with great ingredients.

🌾 Stone-ground flour 🧈 Pure highland butter 🍓 Organic fruits 🌿 No preservatives ♨️ Baked fresh daily
